Overview

What Is Built

An independent solar price comparison platform for the Spanish market. Users enter their phone number, postal code, and electricity bill, and get an instant estimate with pricing, subsidies, financing options, and local installers — no commitment or sales calls required. Spanish phone validation with WhatsApp framing gates every calculation.

Why it matters: Solar clients want to see price estimations upfront — market opacity is a real pain point. This platform gathers market information and customers’ existing proposals, qualifying leads through a structured form (and potentially a chatbot later) before handing them to installers. A great addition to the directories and tuenergiaverde projects, which don’t cover pricing — an important part of the purchase journey.

Ecosystem: Standalone repo (goabeto/precio-solar) with vendored types from abeto-directories. Connects to the same Supabase DB as tuenergiaverde.es. Dedup utility, subsidies API client, and SEO JSON-LD integrated. Luminous Architect design system (warm amber variant). Cross-links to tuenergiaverde.es in footer and installer cards.

Design: Luminous Architect system shared with tuenergiaverde.es — Plus Jakarta Sans 800 headings, tonal surfaces, glassmorphism, ambient shadows. Warm amber accent (#f5a623) distinguishes from tuenergiaverde’s yellow (#facc15). Custom sun+checkmark logo. Each page has its own hero background image with embedded forms.

Product

What Users See

Six tools and a 7-step journey, each addressing a different stage of the solar purchase process. Phone validation gates entry. Partner installers surface as “Top Pick.” Every proposal is stored for market intelligence.

1. Solar Calculator

/

The main entry point. User enters Spanish mobile phone number (required — WhatsApp framing: “verify you’re human, avoid bots, each calculation has a cost to the planet”), postal code, and monthly electricity bill. The system geocodes their location, queries PVGIS for solar irradiation, sizes the system, calculates pricing with regional subsidies, estimates savings and payback, and shows financing options. No sales calls. Privacy guaranteed. Users can request removal.

Built today

  • Spanish mobile validation (6xx/7xx, +34 prefix support)
  • No-sales-calls messaging (communication channel to review)
  • Battery toggle, bill slider (30–300), postal code input
  • PVGIS live irradiation + Nominatim geocoding

2. Financing Comparison

step 3 in flow

Part of the calculator flow. Shows 4 loan products by default, expandable to all 13. Products sorted by TAE, ranging from ICO Linea Verde (2.5% government-backed) to digital lenders like Cofidis and Cetelem. All rates hardcoded with Q2 2026 data (last updated April 10). Weekly research cadence planned via scraping strategy.

Built today

  • 13 loan products with Q2 2026 rates & last_updated field
  • Show 4 by default, “show more” expands to all
  • Integrated into calculator flow (not standalone)
  • Scraping plan documented, automation not yet built

3. Installer Matching

step 4 in flow

Shows 3 local installers by default, expandable to 10. Partner installers appear first with a “Top Pick” badge, mapped by region: Xolary (Madrid, Toledo, Segovia, Avila), ESR / Vira Energy (Barcelona, Valencia), SotySolar (rest of Spain). Users select up to 3 to request quotes. WhatsApp pitch: “we contact on your behalf, preserving your privacy.” Links to tuenergiaverde.es for more options.

Built today

  • Partner installer mapping by region with “Top Pick” badge
  • Show 3 by default, expand to 10
  • Partial name matching for DB variants
  • WhatsApp privacy pitch + tuenergiaverde link
  • Google ratings & review counts displayed

4. Últimos Detalles Antes de Contactar

step 4 — Solicitar

The qualification step that replaces phone calls and WhatsApp back-and-forth. After the user selects installers, they fill a structured form with 7 questions: property address, ownership status, community approval, technician visit availability, timeline, existing proposals, and comments. Plus 3 consent checkboxes (privacy, contact authorization, data processing). This captures everything an installer needs to prepare a real quote — without a single call.

Why this matters: Most solar lead platforms pass a phone number and hope the installer can reach the customer. We capture structured, verified information upfront, making the lead dramatically more valuable. The installer gets a complete brief, and the customer never receives an unwanted call.

Built today

  • 7-question qualification form (QualificationForm component)
  • 3 consent checkboxes (privacy, contact, data)
  • All data stored to solar_journey_leads with full context
  • Email notification to team via Resend on each lead

To improve next

  • Force real address input (autocomplete or map picker, no free text)
  • Validate name field (first + last, no initials or single chars)
  • Cross-validate phone number with name (anti-fraud)
  • Progressive disclosure — reveal questions one by one to reduce drop-off
  • Pre-fill from calculator step (postal code, bill, phone already known)

5. Proposal Review

/revisar-propuesta

Our most differentiated tool — no competitor does this in Spain. A user who received a quote inputs the details (or uploads a photo/file). The system scores price against market data, checks panel/inverter brands, evaluates financing terms, flags missing subsidies. Returns a rating: excellent, good, fair, or “review recommended.” Every submitted proposal is stored in solar_journey_proposals for market intelligence.

Built today

  • All proposals stored to Supabase (fire-and-forget insert)
  • File upload with camera capture on mobile
  • Price + financing + equipment scoring
  • Best subsidy amount for region
  • OCR/photo analysis not yet implemented

Data

Open Data Sources

Public APIs and government data that power (or could power) the platform. Two are live, four more are available for integration.

SourceWhat It ProvidesStatus
PVGIS (EU API) Solar irradiation per location (kWh/kWp/year). Official EU service, free, no key required. Regional fallback built in. Live
Nominatim (OSM) Geocoding postal codes to coordinates + city + region. Free, no key. Fallback: hardcoded coords for all 52 provinces. Live
Subsidies API Real-time address-based subsidy resolution: exact IBI/ICIO bonifications by municipality. Connected to spain-subsidies-research API. Integrated (optional)
Supabase DB Reference pricing, loan products, subsidy programs, installer listings. Shared with tuenergiaverde.es. Live — needs verification
ESIOS (REE) Real-time electricity market prices from Red Eléctrica. Could power savings estimates with actual tariff data instead of user-reported bills. Planned
BDNS (Govt) Base de Datos Nacional de Subvenciones. Official public subsidy database. Could auto-detect new solar subsidy programs. Planned
Catastro (Govt) Property cadastre data including roof area, orientation, and building type. Could estimate installable surface without user input. Planned

Remaining Gaps

Gap

No listing validation badges

The monorepo has AI-powered listing scoring (validation_status, validation_score). Precio Solar doesn’t show quality indicators on installers yet.

Gap

No real-time subsidy resolution

Users enter postal codes but we only show region-level subsidies. The subsidies API can return exact municipal IBI/ICIO bonifications. Quick win — we already geocode.

Gap

No mystery shopping pricing data

Raul’s pipeline collects real pricing from installer email responses. Could feed directly into reference pricing, replacing/validating hardcoded data.

Decisions

5 Decisions to Take Before Further Development

Two decisions made today. Three remain open.

1

Launch scope: all tools live from V1

Decision: Ship everything. Calculator + Financing + Installer Matching + Proposal Review + Guides. Full 7-step flow with qualification form. No phased rollout — the value is in the integrated journey.

Decided
2

Communication channel: WhatsApp, in-app chatbot, or form-only?

The “Conversaciones” step was removed from the flow for now. Several paths are possible: WhatsApp-mediated (we contact installers on the user’s behalf), in-app chatbot (Claude-powered assistant that qualifies and routes), or form-only (the qualification form already captures everything). Each has different cost, UX, and scalability trade-offs. A direction is needed.

Not decided
3

Pricing accuracy: scraping plan documented

Decision: Three-phase strategy. 10 solar calculator providers (weekly) + 13 loan rate providers (monthly). Phase 1: manual spreadsheet. Phase 2: Playwright automation with GitHub Actions. Phase 3: Supabase historical storage + trend analysis. Additionally, every user-submitted proposal feeds market intelligence.

Decided
4

Business model: lead generation first, monetization later

The platform is built for lead capture: calculator → results → financing → installers → qualification → WhatsApp. CPL/CPA model likely. We need to decide: installer referral fees or lead sales? The answer shapes every priority after leads start coming in. For now: bring leads first.

Still open
5

Domain, SEO, and go-to-market

Currently on precio-solar.vercel.app. No custom domain, no Google Analytics, no Search Console. Open questions: preciosolar.es vs subdomain of Tu Energia Verde? Timeline for going public? How to leverage the full pages ecosystem (directories, subsidies, calculators, blog)?

Still open
